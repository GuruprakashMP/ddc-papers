About

DataDrivenChemistryPapers is an automatically updated index of scientific papers in which artificial intelligence, machine learning, or other data-driven techniques are applied to chemistry problems — catalysis, reaction prediction, retrosynthesis, molecular property prediction, ML potentials, spectroscopy, electrochemistry, polymer and materials chemistry, and more.

How it works

Every day an automated pipeline queries open scholarly metadata APIs, removes duplicates, classifies each paper with a chemistry-and-ML relevance model, assigns categories and a relevance score, and regenerates this site.

Data policy

This site stores metadata only: title, authors, journal, date, DOI and link. No abstracts, no full text, no PDFs. Every paper links to the original publisher page. To have a record corrected or removed, contact guruprakash29011999@gmail.com.
